The Firm

Wandira Cynthia & Company Advocates

Practical, efficient, and client-focused legal solutions.

Wandira Cynthia & Company Advocates is a dynamic Kenyan law firm built on integrity, professionalism, and results-oriented legal service.

We provide strategic legal support to individuals, entrepreneurs, corporates, and investors, with strong expertise in property, commercial transactions, and dispute resolution.

Our approach combines sound legal knowledge with a deep understanding of our clients’ commercial and personal needs, ensuring clarity, compliance, and confidence.

Areas of Practice

Comprehensive legal services across core and specialized disciplines.

Core Practice Areas

  • Real Estate & Conveyancing: Sale & purchase, leases, charges, due diligence, securities.
  • Litigation & ADR: Civil & commercial disputes, employment, land, arbitration.
  • Corporate & Commercial Law: Structuring, governance, M&A, compliance.
  • Banking & Finance: Loan documentation, securities, Islamic finance.
  • Tax Law & Advisory: Planning, compliance, KRA disputes, stamp duty.
