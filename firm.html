About Wandira Cynthia & Company Advocates

Providing pragmatic, efficient, and results-driven legal solutions across Kenya and beyond.

Wandira Cynthia & Company Advocates is a dynamic Kenyan law firm committed to delivering practical and client-focused legal solutions. Founded on the principles of integrity, professionalism, and results-oriented service, the firm has grown to serve a diverse clientele including individuals, startups, established businesses, investors, and multinational corporations.

We specialize in providing strategic legal advice that aligns with both the commercial and personal objectives of our clients. Our services span across property and conveyancing, corporate and commercial law, dispute resolution, banking and finance, tax, intellectual property, employment, family law, technology, data protection, and beyond.

Our client-centered approach ensures that complex legal issues are simplified, risks are managed effectively, and business opportunities are optimized. By combining technical expertise with practical insights, we assist our clients in making informed legal decisions while ensuring compliance and protection of their interests.

At Wandira Cynthia & Company Advocates, we are committed to long-term partnerships with our clients, grounded in trust, accountability, and excellence. Beyond legal representation, we serve as dedicated legal advisors throughout the entirety of our clients’ journeys.
